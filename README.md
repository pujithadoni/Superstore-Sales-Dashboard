# Superstore-Sales-Dashboard
This project analyzes retail sales data using the popular Superstore dataset to uncover key business insights related to sales, profit, and customer behavior.  An interactive dashboard was created to help stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.
# Objectives
Analyzed overall sales and profit trends.
Identified top-performing products and categories.
Examined customer segments and purchasing patterns.
Compared regional sales performance.
Generated actionable insights for business growth.
# Dataset
Order details (Order ID, Date, Ship Mode).
Customer information (Customer ID, Segment, Region).
Product details (Category, Sub-category, Product Name).
Sales, Quantity, Discount, Profit.
# Tools Used
Power BI – Dashboard development & data visualization, Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation (ETL), Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) – Data preprocessing and analysis.
# Key Insights from the Dashboard
The business generated ~13M in total sales with 1.47M profit, resulting in an overall profit margin of ~12%.
This indicates a moderate profitability, but there is room for margin improvement.
# Monthly Profit Trends
November and September recorded the highest profits, showing strong seasonal performance.
July and January had the lowest profits, indicating possible off-season periods.
Profit margins remained relatively stable (~10%–13%), suggesting consistent pricing strategy.
# Quantity Sold
Total quantity sold is 178K units, indicating high-volume sales model.                                                                                                 
Insight:
Business relies on volume over high margins, reinforcing the need to control discounts.
# Conclusions
The business is growing steadily, but profit margins remain relatively low (~12%).
Technology products and Consumer segment are the primary revenue drivers.
Seasonality plays a major role, with peak performance in later months.                                                                                              
There are opportunities to:                                                                                                                                         
Improve profit margins (reduce discount impact).                                                                                                                    
Boost underperforming segments (Home Office).                                                                                                                       
Optimize region-wise profitability.
